Definition of Go Green

is a term that refers to the movement and practices aimed at protecting the environment by reducing the negative impact of human activities.

Tips Go Green

  • Reduce Waste: Managing and minimizing the waste produced, including recycling and reusing items.
  • Use Renewable Energy: Transitioning to clean and renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and hydro power.
  • Eco-Friendly Transportation: Choosing more sustainable transportation options, such as public transport, cycling, or walking.
  • Conserve Natural Resources:Using natural resources efficiently and responsibly to ensure their sustainability.
  • Education and Awareness: Increasing understanding of environmental issues and encouraging positive actions in the community.
